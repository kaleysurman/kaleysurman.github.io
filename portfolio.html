About Irie Weekend

Irie Weekend, developed by DJ Irie, is the Irie Foundation’s signature annual benefit. Every summer the Weekend raises money for the Foundation’s mission to positively impact the lives of South Florida’s at-risk youth. All proceeds benefit Foundation programming, as well as to benefit a number of other local and national non-profit organizations. The Weekend features a range of notable VIPs and events including the #InspIRIE Kids Golf Clinic, Irie Foundation #InspIRIE Dinner Gala, Celebrity Golf Tournament, Sea-B-Q Beach Bash pool party concert, Celebrity Brunch and more.

Irie Weekend Presents the Tempo Kids Carnival

One specific event we added to Irie Weekend is the Tempo Kids Carnival. Irie Foundation invites parents and kids to attend the Irie Weekend XIV inaugural Tempo Kids Carnival. On Sunday, July 1, 2018 from 10 a.m. - 2 p.m. located at Bayfront Park in Brickell. This festival includes carnival games, rides, face painting and food trucks. A special surprise performance by a celebrity artist begins at 12 p.m. Limited edition Irie Weekend merchandise are available for purchase at the Melody Market tent at the event. Come join us at the inaugural Tempo Kids Carnival!
